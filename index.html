About Us

Your space is any space. Every space. You don’t have to have an office, you just need a desk. (And coffee. Always coffee.)

But that space isn’t just about yourself, it’s about your business; about others. Advertising, collaboration, brainstorming - where does all that happen in your space? You need something more than just digital space, you need a white space.

 

Let’s DrawAttention.

Slap this on your laptop lid (or anywhere, for that matter), and you’ve turned that blank space into everything you need right now and more: a billboard, a white board, a scratch pad, and an invitation. It’s the perfect solution:

  • Low power, sustainable display technology
  • Infinite font and color support
  • Leverages enterprise best practices found in every office in the world
  • Connect multiple spaces together wirelessly (although it works best when they’re pushed together)

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Is this a real product?
    • Yes, yes it is.
  2. Will this work with any kind of marker?
    • With a Sharpie we can only guarantee it will work once. Dry-Erase? At least a couple more times than that.
  3. What if it won’t fit my laptop?
    • Then we advise the same process as when buying holiday sweatpants: buy big and trim down later.
  4. Can I put this on my tablet or phone?
    • Yep, more than one! We recommend The Contrarian XL + Xacto knife + a ruler + friends + booze AFTER you cut.
  5. Can I take it off later?
    • That’s an awfully personal question, but yes - yes you can.
